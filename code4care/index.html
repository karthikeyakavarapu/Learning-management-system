The Problem

Traffic Kills. Not Just Accidents.

In India alone, over 1.5 lakh people die annually in road accidents — many reaching hospitals too late. The root cause? Broken emergency routing.

The Golden Hour Crisis

Medical emergencies have a critical 60-minute window. Beyond it, survival rates plummet exponentially. Yet, the average ambulance response time in Indian metro cities is 28 minutes — nearly half the golden hour squandered in traffic.

Our Solution

AI That Thinks Faster
Than Traffic Builds Up.

CODE4CARE is a full-stack intelligent emergency response platform combining ML severity triage, live traffic routing, hospital capacity matching, and predictive analytics in real time.

01
🔍
Patient Severity Triage
Our ML model analyzes 14 real-time vitals — heart rate, SpO₂, blood pressure, GCS score — and classifies the patient as Low, Medium, or Critical in under 3 seconds.
02
🏥
Smart Hospital Matching
Based on severity, the system queries all nearby hospitals for bed availability, ICU capacity, and specialist presence — updated every 30 seconds in real time.
03
🗺️
Dynamic Route Optimization
Powered by Google Maps Traffic API + proprietary ML prediction, the system plots the fastest corridor — recalculating every 45 seconds as conditions evolve.
04
📣
Pre-Arrival Hospital Alert
The destination hospital receives an automated alert with ETA, patient vitals, and condition summary — so the trauma team is ready the moment the ambulance arrives.
Core Features

Built for the Emergency Room
of the Future

🧠
ML Severity Classifier
A trained Random Forest model processes 14 patient parameters — ECG patterns, GCS, pulse oximetry — achieving 94.2% classification accuracy across three severity tiers. Inference time: <3 seconds.
94.2% Accuracy
📡
Real-Time GPS Fleet Tracking
Every ambulance broadcasts its position every 5 seconds via embedded IoT GPS modules. Central command has sub-10m positioning accuracy for the entire fleet simultaneously, with zero latency alerts.
±10m Accuracy
🗺️
Live Traffic Route Engine
Integrated with Google Maps Traffic API and HERE Maps. Considers speed, congestion levels, signal wait times, and live accident reports to compute the true minimum ETA route — not just shortest distance.
Dynamic Rerouting
🏥
Hospital Capacity Sync
Partner hospitals push live bed availability, ICU occupancy, and specialist schedules every 60 seconds. Critical patients are never routed to overwhelmed facilities — the system knows before the dispatcher does.
Live Sync
🚦
Traffic Signal Priority Control
Smart city IoT integration enables remote green-light corridors to be triggered for critical ambulances — programmatically overriding signal cycles to reduce intersection wait time by up to 80%.
Smart City Ready
📊
Predictive Demand Analytics
Historical emergency data is analyzed to pre-position ambulances in high-probability zones before incidents occur. The LSTM model predicts demand hotspots 45 minutes in advance with 82% accuracy.
Predictive AI
